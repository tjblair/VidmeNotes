1. A bratty kid finds a spider and pulls off 1/4 of it's legs. How many
   legs does the poor spider have left?

2. 56 ravens are being chased by witches but only 1/8 of the ravens belong
   to them. How many of the ravens are wild?

3. There is a party at a haunted house with 12 zombies and 15 demons. 
   Half of the zombies and 1/3 of the demons leave before midnight. 
   How many monsters are left?
